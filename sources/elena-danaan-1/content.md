Elena Danaan is a French-born author, archaeologist, and self-described contactee and experiencer who claims ongoing interactions with extraterrestrial beings. She asserts that she serves as an emissary of the "Galactic Federation of Worlds" through her contact with an alleged extraterrestrial named Thor Han Eredyon. Danaan shares her experiences through books, interviews, and online platforms, aiming to inform the public about extraterrestrial involvement in Earth's affairs.
Early life and education[[edit](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&veaction=edit§ion=1) | [edit source](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&action=edit§ion=1)]
Born in France, Danaan graduated in Fine Arts in Paris and completed postgraduate studies in archaeology at the Louvre University.[[1]](#cite_note-about-1) She worked as a field archaeologist for twenty years, including eight years in Egypt as an engineer epigraphist for the Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS), working at sites such as Karnak, Luxor, Cairo, Dendera, and the Valley of the Kings. Upon returning to France, she was assigned to the University of Toulouse. Danaan also completed studies in Druidry, shamanism, energy, and sound healing, and currently works as a spiritual practitioner.[[1]](#cite_note-about-1)
Claims and Experiences[[edit](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&veaction=edit§ion=2) | [edit source](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&action=edit§ion=2)]
Alleged contacts[[edit](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&veaction=edit§ion=3) | [edit source](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&action=edit§ion=3)]
Danaan reports that her experiences with extraterrestrial beings began in childhood. She claims to have been abducted by Zeta Reticuli small Grays and subsequently rescued by a crew from the Galactic Federation of Worlds, including a being named [Thor Han Eredyon](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Thor_Han_Eredyon&action=edit&redlink=1) from the [Pleiades](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/wiki/Pleiades).[[2]](#cite_note-giftfromthestars-2) These experiences are detailed in her book A Gift From The Stars: Extraterrestrial Contacts and Guide of Alien Races.
"My mission is to share the messages and knowledge imparted to me by these benevolent beings," she states in her book.[[2]](#cite_note-giftfromthestars-2)
Publications[[edit](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&veaction=edit§ion=4) | [edit source](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&action=edit§ion=4)]
She has authored several books on extraterrestrial contact and related subjects, including:
- A Gift From The Stars: Extraterrestrial Contacts and Guide of Alien Races (2020)
[[2]](#cite_note-giftfromthestars-2) - We Will Never Let You Down: Encounters with Val Thor and Journeys Beyond Earth (2021)
[[3]](#cite_note-wwnlyd-3) - The Seeders: The Return Of The Gods (2022)
[[4]](#cite_note-seeders-4) - Area 51: Conversations with Insider Stephen Chua (2023)
[[5]](#cite_note-area51-5) - Encyclopedia Galactica Volume I: From Andromeda to Canis Minor (2024)
Her works have been translated into multiple languages and have gained a following among readers interested in ufology and extraterrestrial contact.[[1]](#cite_note-about-1)

Danaan frequently shares her experiences through:
YouTube Videos: Hosting a channel where she discusses her alleged encounters and offers insights on extraterrestrial matters.[[6]](#cite_note-youtube-6)
Interviews: Appearing on various podcasts, radio shows, and conferences related to ufology and spirituality.
Reception[[edit](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&veaction=edit§ion=6) | [edit source](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&action=edit§ion=6)]
Support[[edit](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&veaction=edit§ion=7) | [edit source](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&action=edit§ion=7)]
Some members of the ufology community view her accounts as genuine and insightful, contributing to discussions on extraterrestrial life and contact.
Criticism[[edit](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&veaction=edit§ion=8) | [edit source](/web/20251114143106/https://en.ikwipedia.org/index.php?title=Elena_Danaan&action=edit§ion=8)]

Debunking Efforts: Critics point out inconsistencies and the absence of empirical evidence supporting her narratives.
